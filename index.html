Frequently Asked Questions

Haan, aap apni raw video files Google Drive, WeTransfer ya Dropbox ke zariye share kar sakte hain. Agar aap faceless AI vids banwana chahte hain, toh bas script/concept de dein, visual assets mai khud generate kar lunga.

Shorts aur Reels ke drafts aam taur par 24 se 72 ghante (1-3 days) ke andar deliver ho jate hain. Long-form videos me editing complexity ke hisab se 3-5 days lag sakte hain.

Mai professional editing ke liye Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects aur relevant AI generation tools use karta hoon taaki dynamic cuts aur high-retention transitions generate kiye ja sakein.

Basic package me 2 rounds revisions ke milte hain. Standard aur Premium packages me jab tak aap fully satisfied nahi ho jate, tab tak unlimited revisions bilkul free hain. Hum direct WhatsApp par coordinate karte hain.

Aap payment UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) ke zariye kar sakte hain. Payment tabhi karni hoti hai jab aap final approved draft dekh kar ok kar dete hain.
